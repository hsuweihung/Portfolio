Summary

Rock Panda started as a project during the Hsinchu X Meichu Hackathon in partnership with Line Corporation. The project was student-centric, focusing on addressing common academic needs, such as exam preparation and course material assistance. Our team developed an intelligent Linebot using RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) technology. This Linebot serves as a digital assistant, allowing students to access past exam questions and generate similar ones. It also supports anonymous inquiries when students have questions.The project received first place from Line Corporation and an overall first-place award in the competition.
